Frequently Asked Question

How do I get started?

We’re so glad you’re here! To get started, click on the sign-up button that says ‘Join The Free Trial’ and fill in the form that appears. Our team will reach out to you within 48 hours to match you with your perfect companion. If you need any assistance, please reach out to us at +91 7738896930 from Monday to Friday between 10 AM to 6 PM. We’re always here to help.

Is this a paid service? Why?

Your first month with a Goodfellow is entirely free. If you are happy, you can continue with a paid subscription. If you do not wish to continue, there will be no money charged. Your Goodfellows have been vetted for empathy and have an affinity towards elderlies, but like any other graduate, they would like to feel secure about their finances and do their best for you. To give them dignified, respectable salaries for their time invested, this is a paid service. This also ensures their longstanding association with us in order to fulfill an enduring companionship with you.
